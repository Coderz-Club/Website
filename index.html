Our Mission: Let's Build the Future, Together!

Hey, it's Theo here, and at Coderz, we're all about learning tech the right way – by doing it! We're building a community where teens like us can:

  • Learn from each other: Forget boring lectures! We believe the best way to learn is by sharing what we know with our friends.
  • Get real help from teens who get it: We've got experienced teens who've been there, done that, and are ready to help you level up.
  • Dive into what you love: Whether it's coding, game dev, or anything tech-related, we're here to help you explore your passions.
  • Create awesome projects: Build apps, games, websites, robots - if you can dream it, we'll help you make it!
  • Join a supportive community: Make friends who share your interests and collaborate on exciting tech challenges.

You don't need to be a tech genius or have fancy gear to join us. Just bring your curiosity and get ready to have fun! We'll provide the tools and the know-how.

Our meetups are informal, hands-on, and designed to spark creativity. No pressure, no grades—just the joy of building and learning together.

(and as a bonus, get LOTS of stickers!!!)

Ready to join us? Check out our upcoming events or drop by our Discord server!
