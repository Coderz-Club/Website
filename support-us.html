Setting the Record Straight

A common misconception is that we're fully funded by Hack Club and our schools - we are not! While Hack Club provides us with resources and banking services, the grants they provide are for rewards only.

For everything else - from equipment to learning materials - we rely entirely on generous donors like you. Your support is what makes our programs possible!

100% of our equipment, supplies, and learning resources come ONLY from DONATIONS!

Where Your Donations Go

Every dollar you donate goes directly to empowering students with the tools they need to build the future

Hardware & Equipment

We purchase microcontrollers, Orpheus Picos, Arduino kits, and other hardware that students can use to build amazing projects.

Software Subscriptions

Access to professional development tools and learning platforms that would otherwise be inaccessible to many students.

Learning Resources

Technical books, online courses, and specialized learning materials that help students master new skills.

Community Events

Hackathons, workshops, and collaborative coding sessions that bring our tech community together to learn and build.
